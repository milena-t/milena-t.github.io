General information

So that I don't have to repeat myself every time: all pieces are sanded and primed with two layers of gesso. I have the cheap acrylic paints from Clas Ohlsson in primary colors, plus green and dark brown and I mixed all shades from these. All pieces are sealed with the matte indoor varnish from panduro.
