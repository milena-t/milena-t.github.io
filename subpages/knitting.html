Beetle sweater

The base sweater pattern is a raglan from petiteknit (Monday sweater), and I originally created the repeating color chart in excel myself, you can download it here. Unfortunately, I miscounted some stitches when casting on the neckline, so the yolk is pretty small, making it kind of tight under the armpits. Still wearable, but essentially zero ease.

I promise I finished weaving in the ends after taking this picture. It is also unblocked here, but I didn't stretch it so it looks about the same.
